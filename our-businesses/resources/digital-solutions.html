Digital Solutions

Explore user-friendly apps, planning resources and portals designed to help you stay organized, manage and track ongoing projects and confidently design, research and plan for future ones.

Apps

Manage your projects with apps that offer real-time tracking, advanced simulations and easy access to key resources.

SMARTCast

Our app, which is connected to sensors in your concrete, provides real-time strength and temperature data to help you track progress and keep your project on schedule.

Download PDF

 

SMARTFlow

With digital simulations, our app measures concrete flowability and matches it to the appropriate pump, reducing costs and delays, and eliminating trial waste.

Download PDF

 

SMARTherm

Allows you to simulate the temperature of large concrete elements, helping to optimize concrete mixes and avoid risks from heat of hydration without real-size tests.
