Advanced roofing for commercial builds

At Amrize, we provide comprehensive solutions from foundation to rooftop — and everything in between. With more than 40 years of commercial roofing experience, we offer solutions that meet industry needs for every building, system and budget. Innovative products such as Elevate self-adhering roofing membranes with SecureBond technology™, Duro-Last PVC membranes, Malarkey smog reducing shingles and Gaco waterproofing ensure cutting-edge durability and design for every commercial roofing project.
