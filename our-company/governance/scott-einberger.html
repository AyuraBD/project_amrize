Scott Einberger

Investor Relations Officer

Scott Einberger serves as Amrize Investor Relations Officer, a role he held for Holcim’s North American business from August 2024 to June 2025. 

 

Prior to joining Holcim, Mr. Einberger served as Head of Investor Relations of Jones Lang LaSalle from 2021 to July 2024. Prior to that, Mr. Einberger held several positions at US Foods, including Director of Investor Relations, Director of Accounting Special Projects, Senior Manager of Finance, and Senior Project Manager from 2010 to 2021. 

 

Mr. Einberger holds an MBA in Accounting and Finance from Loyola University in Chicago, Illinois, and a Bachelor of Finance and Management from the University of Colorado. Mr. Einberger is a Certified Management Accountant.
